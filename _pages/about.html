.Who am I

I'm a Portuguese expat living in the UK for the past 5 years. I'm currently working for an airline as cabin crew and learning how to code in my free time.

I've always been interested in coding and creating my own programs. After completing the online course Intro to CS and programming with python from MIT at edx.org I've decided that it was time to dedicate my free time to learn more about coding and computer science.

I've been studying on my own using books, online courses and everything else I can get my hands into. I'm currently digging deeper into python, mathematics and algorithms. MY goal is to become a good developer and work in challenging new things.

I also enjoy travelling and meeting new people and new cultures. I've been to 13 different countries so far, seen over 65 new cities and stepped on 3 different continents.
